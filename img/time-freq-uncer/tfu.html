Time-Frequency Uncertainty

Theorem

Given a window function w(t), define the time and frequency bandwidth as:

Time BW:σt(w)=∫t2|w(t)|2dtFreq BW:σu(w)=∫u2|W(u)|2du

Then,

σt(w)⋅σu(w)≥116π2

σt(w)⋅σu(w)=[∫t2|w(t)|2dt][∫u2|W(u)|2du]=[∫t2|w(t)|2dt][∫|1j2πddtw(t)|2dt]=14π2∥tw(t)∥2⋅∥dw(t)dt∥2
